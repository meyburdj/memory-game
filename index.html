Kitty Defender!

The Rise of Meowthum Von Laserbirth

Long after the final laser pointer was moved by human hands, cats continued to relentlessly chase dots of light wherever the could be found. Following the "Litter-Box" events of 2276, the cyborg-kitty faction claimed dominion over all over cats with the birth of Meothum Von Laserbirth. Able to command his fellow cats with a single gaze, Meothum rapidly rose to power.
In a final effort to liberate catdom from this despotic rule, the ancient order of smol cats began seeking out the legendary six smol cats of old. These cats, the cutest that had ever been spawned, had their souls separated and placed in cryogenic cardboard boxes. Help reunite their adorable souls so that they may vanquish Meowthum!
